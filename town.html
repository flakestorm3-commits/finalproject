Hunters' Burrow

The Hospital

The local hospital run by Dr. Hamilton. The citizens of the town go there mostly for normal checkups, terrible injuries rarely occuring in Hunters' Burrows. It also doubles as a senior home, having a wing for the elderly to recieve care.

Burrows Library

The Burrows Library is one of the town's oldest buildings behind Town Hall, being the second place built once the town was established years ago. Rumor has it that the reason for this is because the founding men and women of the town had important texts that were at risk of ruin due to unpredictable weather at the time and a vote led to the library being built next, people sleeping in the Town Hall during construction. Claire Holmes works here as one of the librarians.

The Town Hall of Hunter's Burrow

This is the Town Hall of Hunter's Burrow and it is one of the oldest buildings, having recieved many renovations over the year. The current mayor, Mayor Lestra, and his family live there. People do business there with their taxes and this is where the citizens of the town gather to vote for or against laws as well as decide on something together.

Abandoned Mansion

This is an old manor that some rumored belonged to rich noble family in the past. However, the reason for it's abandonment is lost to time, leaving behind an empty manor that is slowly crumbling day by day. People rarely go near it, the building considered condemned and the mayor having banned anyone from going near it for saftey.

Not that it stopped anyone.

Burrows' Groceries

The local grocery store that caters the most to what customers need in the town. Everyone in town shops here for their food, medicene, and odds and ends they need. The shop has seen better days, needing renovations, but the renovations keep being pushed back as other prooblems crop up in town. However, the owner, Mrs. Nit, makes do to keep the store afloat and watch out for her employees.
